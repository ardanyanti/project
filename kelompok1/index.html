Latest and the debut album

Fábula is Mahalini's first studio album released on January 23, 2023. The album contains 10 songs including four previously released singles, namely "Melawan Restu", "Sisa Rasa", "Kisah Sempurna", and a new version of Mahalini's debut single in 2015 entitled "Bawa Dia Kembali". Although initially only available digitally, the album was later released physically on CD in boxset format on October 14, 2023, coinciding with Mahalini's solo concert entitled Fabula 2.0.


Fábula is the most popular Indonesian album on the Spotify platform with a total of more than 1 billion streams as of December 2023 Through this album, Mahalini was nominated for two categories at the 2023 Indonesian Music Awards and won Best Pop Album.

Mencintaimu (From : 2nd Miracle in Cell No. 7 )



“Mencintaimu” by Mahalini is a heartfelt reinterpretation of the renowned Indonesian love ballad originally performed by Kris Dayanti during the 2000s. Written by Bebi Romeo and given a fresh orchestral arrangement by Andi Rianto, Mahalini breathes new emotion into this beloved song. Released in December 2024, her cover was selected as the official soundtrack for the Indonesian film 2nd Miracle in Cell No. 7 (sequel to the acclaimed 2022 film) and adds depth and poignancy to its storytelling. With her soulful vocals and modern pop sensibility, Mahalini’s version resonates with themes of unwavering devotion, longing, and eternal love.
